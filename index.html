Who am I?

I'm a Portuguese student of 21 years, my name is Catarina. I'm taking a degree in visual arts and technology. From an early age I was interested in illustration and comics, much of it due to its liveliness surrounded by the nature of the Sintra mountains and the brilliant animations I've been following since childhood. I get a lot of inspiration from the studio Ghibli movies, from comics like the Corto Maltese, and from other Japanese animations.

What I do

I completed the high school in the course of visual arts in the year in the secondary of Santa Maria. I attended a year of illustrarion and comics in 2015 at Ar.co and finally I'm currently finishin my degree in visual arts and technologies at Escola Superior de Educação de Lisboa.
